=== ONBOARDING CHECKLIST (NEW EMPLOYEES) ===
The following should be completed within your first two weeks:

Week 1:
- Complete I-9 and tax withholding forms in Workday (HR system)
- Enroll in benefits (health, dental, vision) — enrollment window is 30 days from start date
- Set up direct deposit in Workday
- Complete mandatory compliance training: Code of Conduct, Data Privacy, Anti-Harassment (due by end of Week 1)
- Receive equipment from IT; complete device setup with IT Help Desk (help@acmecorp.com)
- Schedule 1:1 with your manager (Priya Nair for Payments team members)
- Get access to core tools: GitHub, Jira, Confluence, Slack, Grafana (submit IT access request via help@acmecorp.com)

Week 2:
- Complete Security Awareness Training (assigned in KnowBe4)
- Review and sign the Acceptable Use Policy and Confidentiality Agreement (sent via DocuSign)
- Complete PCI DSS Awareness Training (required for all Payments team members)
- Schedule introductory 1:1s with each team member
- Attend your first sprint planning session with the team

HR contact for onboarding questions: onboarding@acmecorp.com
IT Help Desk: help@acmecorp.com or Slack #it-help

=== TIME OFF POLICIES ===

Paid Time Off (PTO):
- Full-time employees accrue 15 days (120 hours) of PTO per year in Years 1–3
- Employees with 3+ years of tenure accrue 20 days (160 hours) per year
- PTO accrues monthly and is available to use as it accrues
- Maximum PTO carryover at year-end: 5 days. Unused PTO above that does not roll over.
- To request PTO: submit in Workday at least 3 business days in advance for up to 3 days;
  at least 2 weeks in advance for 4+ consecutive days
- Manager approval required for all PTO requests

Sick Leave:
- 10 days (80 hours) of sick leave per year, non-accruing (full amount available January 1)
- Sick leave does not roll over year to year
- No documentation required for absences of 1–2 days; doctor's note required for 3+ consecutive days
- Sick leave may be used for personal illness or to care for an immediate family member

Holidays:
- Acme Corp observes 11 federal holidays plus 2 floating holidays per year
- Floating holidays can be used for any purpose and must be used within the calendar year
- Holiday schedule published annually in Confluence (search: "Acme Corp Holiday Calendar")

Parental Leave:
- 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for primary caregivers (birth, adoption, or foster placement)
- 6 weeks of fully paid parental leave for secondary caregivers
- Leave can be taken any time within 12 months of the qualifying event
- Notify HR and your manager at least 30 days in advance when possible

Bereavement Leave:
- 5 days paid leave for immediate family (spouse/partner, child, parent, sibling)
- 3 days paid leave for extended family (grandparent, in-law, aunt/uncle)
- Additional unpaid leave may be arranged with manager approval

=== BENEFITS OVERVIEW ===

Health Insurance:
- Three plan options: PPO (high coverage), HDHP with HSA, and HMO
- Acme Corp covers 80% of premium for employee; 60% for dependents
- Open enrollment is in November for the following year
- Qualifying life events (marriage, birth, etc.) allow mid-year changes — notify HR within 30 days

Dental and Vision:
- Dental: Acme Corp covers 70% of premium; includes preventive, basic, and major services
- Vision: Annual eye exam and allowance for frames/lenses or contacts covered

401(k) Retirement Plan:
- Acme Corp matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of salary
- Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years
- Enroll or change contributions at any time via Fidelity NetBenefits

Other Benefits:
- $2,000/year learning & development stipend (courses, certifications, conferences)
- $100/month remote work stipend (internet, home office supplies)
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): free confidential counseling and support services
- Commuter benefits: pre-tax transit and parking accounts

=== CODE OF CONDUCT ===

Acme Corp expects all employees to:
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and professionalism
- Maintain confidentiality of company and customer data at all times
- Report conflicts of interest to HR or your manager promptly
- Not engage in harassment, discrimination, or retaliation of any kind
- Use company systems and data only for authorized business purposes
- Comply with all applicable laws, including data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- Report suspected violations of policy or law via the Ethics Hotline (anonymous):
  ethics@acmecorp.com or 1-800-555-0199

Violations of the Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

=== PERFORMANCE REVIEWS ===
- Formal reviews are conducted twice per year: June (mid-year check-in) and December (annual review)
- Reviews are completed in Workday
- Employees submit a self-assessment; managers provide written feedback and a rating
- Ratings: Exceeds Expectations / Meets Expectations / Needs Improvement
- Compensation adjustments are tied to the annual December review cycle
- Promotions can occur outside of the review cycle with VP-level approval

=== WORKPLACE POLICIES ===

Remote Work:
- Payments team operates on a hybrid model: 2 days in office per week (Tuesday and Thursday)
- Remote work outside the standard schedule requires manager approval
- Employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in their local timezone
- Travel to a different country for more than 30 days requires HR and Legal approval

Expense Reimbursement:
- Submit expenses within 30 days of the expense date via Concur
- Manager approval required for expenses over $100
- Receipts required for all expenses over $25
- Pre-approval required for any expense over $500

Equipment:
- Acme Corp provides a laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse to all full-time employees
- Equipment must be returned upon separation from the company
- For repairs or replacements, contact IT Help Desk (help@acmecorp.com)
